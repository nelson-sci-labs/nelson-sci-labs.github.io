Interpretation & Reporting

Omics datasets are large and can seem overwhelming. Bringing a project through the data acquisition phase can be quite expensive. Therefore, it is best to extract as much value as possible from the resulting data. Furthermore, communicating relevant features in high-quality reports and manuscripts is essential. Each study is unique in its data and goals and deserves individual, custom attention. We at Nelson Scientific Labs are keenly focused on these phases of systems biology projects and are particularly eager to help bring your study to fruition.

Comprehensive Data Analytics and Interpretation

  • Customized analyses and interpretation by our team of experts using state-of-the-art molecule identification and biochemical pathway tools.
  • Identification of candidate features for deeper analyses with analysis plan augmentation recommendations.
  • Statistical analyses (descriptive statistics, univariate and multivariate inferential statistics, parametric and non-parametric hypothesis tests, etc.).
  • Complete methodological transparency and data access with optional data hosting and archiving.
  • Ongoing support and consultation on how to address new hypotheses generated by the concluding project.

Reporting and Communication

  • Manuscript and report preparation, from as little as Materials & Methods essentials to full manuscript authoring.
  • Custom tables, charts, graphs, and figures, made to order.
  • Poster and platform presentation authoring.
  • Proofreading and editing.
