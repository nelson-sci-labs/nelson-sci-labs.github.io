Maximizing the value of your omics data through comprehensive analysis, interpretation, and communication.

Customized Analysis and Interpretation

Omics datasets are vast and can appear overwhelming. The data acquisition phase of a project can be costly, making it crucial to extract as much value as possible from the resulting data. Furthermore, effectively communicating the relevant findings in high-quality reports and manuscripts is essential. Each study is unique in its data and objectives, requiring individualized attention. At Nelson Scientific Labs, we specialize in these critical phases of systems biology projects and are eager to help bring your study to fruition.


Our comprehensive data analytics and interpretation services include:

  • Customized analyses and interpretation conducted by our team of experts using cutting-edge molecule identification and biochemical pathway tools.
  • Detection of candidate features for in-depth analyses and recommendations for analysis plan augmentation.
  • Statistical analyses, including descriptive statistics, univariate and multivariate inferential statistics, parametric and non-parametric hypothesis tests, and more.
  • Complete methodological transparency and data access with optional data hosting and archiving services.
  • Ongoing support and consultation to address new hypotheses generated by the project's conclusions.

Professional Reporting and Communication

Effectively communicating your research findings is critical to ensuring your work has the desired impact. Our team of experienced scientific writers and editors can help you craft clear, concise, and compelling reports and manuscripts to convey the results of your study. From drafting essential materials and methods to full manuscript authoring, we provide a range of services tailored to your needs.

Our reporting and communication services include:

  • Manuscript and report preparation, ranging from Materials & Methods essentials to full manuscript authoring.
  • Custom tables, charts, graphs, and figures designed to clearly present your data.
  • Poster and platform presentation authoring for conferences and symposia.
  • Proofreading and editing to ensure accuracy, clarity, and consistency in your documents.
