Study Design & Consulting

Project success begins with a carefully thought-out analytic roadmap. At Nelson Scientific Labs, we understand that designing a study can seem daunting. This is why we not only offer analytic services but also assistance with planning your studies. We are experienced in designing and executing systems biology research projects. Let us help you determine your best possible analytic plan and overall study design.

Our experts at Nelson Scientific Labs are happy to help you answer a variety of important planning-stage questions before you begin your project. We are also ready to jump in with you at any step along the way.

  • What do I expect to have upon completion of my project?
  • Is a systems biology approach good for answering my research question?
  • What biological model(s) should I use in my study?
  • What kinds of samples should I collect? How many should I collect?
  • How will I store and prepare my samples?
  • Which analytic platform(s) will provide the best data for answering my research question?
  • What kind of data will I get after my samples have been processed?
  • What should I do with my data? How should I analyze it?
  • If I find something interesting, what then? Are there any identification or validation follow-up options?
  • What's the best way to communicate my findings?

These questions can be quite intimidating, even for the experienced researcher. Your friends at Nelson Scientific Labs are eager to help you answer these questions and put your mind at ease. Don’t stress over it—give us a call!
